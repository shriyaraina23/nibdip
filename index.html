Our Features

Timetable

The weekly time table will also be a part of the website’s feature and the teachers have to confirm the lectures prior the time. The students will get direct notifications for confirmed as well as cancelled  lectures..

Chatroom

The website will also provide the users with a “Chat Room” where they can freely discuss and talk with their fellow mates.

Notes

Our website will provide the teachers a space for uploading notes for the students from where the students can save the files and it is worth noting that they can easily sort and manage the files on their “profile page”.

User friendly

We plan to create a student friendly website which will work as an intermediate between the students and the teachers.
